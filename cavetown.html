Cavetown

Cavetown was born on 15 December 1998 in Oxford, England. Their music interest was inspired by his father, David Skinner, who was a musicologist and choir director, and taught him how to play the acoustic guitar at eight years old. Their mother is a professional music teacher and flautist. They were raised in Cambridge and educated at Parkside Community College from 2010-2015 and Hills Road Sixth Form College from 2015-2017.

Robin Daniel Skinner (born 15 December 1998), known professionally as Cavetown (sometimes stylized in all lowercase), is an English singer-songwriter, record producer, and YouTuber. His style blends elements of indie rock, indie pop and bedroom pop with mellow, gentle ukulele ballads. As of December 2023, he had amassed over 6.8 million monthly streamers on Spotify. His YouTube channel, which he began in November 2012, sits at 2.21 million subscribers and 523 million video views as of December 2023. Skinner released his fourth studio album, Sleepyhead, in March 2020 and his fifth, Worm Food, in November 2022.

Artist Record:

  • 2015-2020
    1. Skinner began his YouTube channel in November 2012 and uploaded his first video, an original song named "Haunted Lullaby", in October 2013. The first song Skinner wrote and recorded was "Rain" in 2013. Soon after, Skinner began releasing music to Bandcamp with his first album, Everything Is Made of Clouds, at the age of 14. He released Gd Vibes, Nervous Friends // Pt. 1, Balance (as brother) and Everything is Made of Stars on Bandcamp over the following two years. Critics expressed that he felt "the acceptance of ignorance" on Gd Vibes. Skinner released his debut single "This is Home" in August 2015, before releasing his debut self-titled album in November 2015. Critics described the album Cavetown as "an eclectic mix of acoustic and electronic". Skinner continued to publish covers of songs onto his YouTube channel from artists such as Pinegrove, Twenty One Pilots and Joji.
